Michelle's Journey

A Career in Film and Technology

Before diving into the world of technology, I spent years working on film sets, learning the art of storytelling, and collaborating with talented creatives. My background as a theatre major fostered my love for production and gave me a deep appreciation for the power of creative collaboration.

Even today, those experiences influence my work in tech. I approach every project with the same attention to detail and passion for innovation that I learned on set. Now, when I'm not immersed in tech, you can find me hiking with my husband and our charcoal Labrador, often exploring the scenic beauty of Malibu Beach.

Passion for Customer Success

My passion lies in helping clients achieve their goals. I thrive on building relationships with customers, understanding their needs, and driving success through collaboration and innovative solutions. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working closely with amazing clients, ensuring they get the most value out of the products and services they use.

Customer success, for me, is more than just support—it's about truly partnering with clients to ensure they are set up for success and reaching their full potential. This requires ongoing communication, training, and strategic planning. I love seeing the positive impact our solutions can have on a client’s business, and I take pride in being a part of that journey.

Becoming More Technical

In my role, I’m constantly looking to enhance my technical knowledge to better support my customers. By working closely with our solutions architect and product teams, I’m learning the inner workings of our platform and solutions so I can provide deeper insights and technical support. I enjoy diving into the technical side of things, from understanding product architecture to exploring how integrations work, and I look forward to continuing to expand this knowledge.

This desire to become more technical comes from my commitment to providing the best possible support to my customers. I believe that having a strong technical foundation allows me to better anticipate challenges and offer proactive solutions. My goal is to bridge the gap between technical complexity and customer success, ensuring that clients feel confident and empowered in using our products.
