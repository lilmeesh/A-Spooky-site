Who is Meesh?

About Me

Hi there! I’m Michelle, but everyone calls me Meesh. You could say I’m a blend of Barbie and tech—passionate about both creativity and innovation. I’ve found my way into the tech world, but it was my love for film that first guided me to discover who I am. There’s a certain glamour to storytelling, whether it’s on screen or through code, and I embrace it all!

A Barbie in Film

I used to spend my days on film sets, working alongside some incredible storytellers and creatives. My background as a theatre major helped me fall in love with the art of production. These experiences taught me the importance of detail, creativity, and collaboration. Even now, as I dive deeper into the world of technology, I carry the lessons from those days with me.

These days, you’re likely to find me enjoying a hike with my husband and our charcoal Labrador. We love Malibu Beach, where the waves meet the sunset—it's pure magic. Malibu isn’t just for Barbie, it’s also for Meesh!

Favorite Movies

I’m all about films that are unique and stylish, just like Barbie’s outfits. My top favorites include:

  • Beetlejuice – A quirky, colorful masterpiece that inspires me to think outside the box.
  • Double Indemnity – A sleek, classic noir that has a dark, glamorous edge.
  • Sabrina – Audrey Hepburn in her most iconic and elegant role. The fashion, the grace—it’s unforgettable!

Barbie’s Family First

Just like Barbie has her close-knit group of friends, family is my biggest source of strength. My sister, whom I lovingly call "Peanut," is one of my biggest inspirations. She just qualified for the Boston Marathon—a true Barbie-level achievement! Her determination pushes me to keep striving for my own goals.

A Tech-Savvy Barbie

While Barbie has her dreamhouse, I have my dream in tech. A few years ago, I started a podcast with a friend, and that’s where my journey into coding began. We’ve been building our website, and with the help of Vercel, I’m excited to continue updating it with new episodes every week. The possibilities are endless, and I love a challenge!
